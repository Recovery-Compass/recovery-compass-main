### 2. PROPERTY FRAUD INVESTIGATION WORKFLOW
**Primary Target:** FL-150 Income & Expense Declaration perjury
**Success Rate:** Near 100% when property ownership exists

**Investigation Steps:**
1. FL-150 Analysis
   - Question 11: "Do you own real estate?" (if NO but records show YES = perjury)
   - Question 13: Business interests (check Secretary of State LLC filings)

2. LLC Ownership Tracing
   - CA Secretary of State Business Entity Search (free)
   - LA County Assessor Property Tax Portal (free)
   - Cross-reference acquisition dates with marriage date

3. Sham Lien Detection
   - Review recorded deeds of trust
   - Red flags: family member lender, zero payments, loan = equity value

4. Post-Filing Dissipation Check
   - Compare filing date with transfer recording dates
   - Any post-filing transfers = ATRO violation = contempt
