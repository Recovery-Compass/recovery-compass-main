## Case Law Support

### *Marriage of Borson* (1986)
Establishes attorney's right to seek fees from opposing party, but fees must still be reasonable.

### *Christian Research Institute v. Alnor* (2008)
Block billing prevents court from assessing reasonableness; justifies fee reduction.

### *Ketchum v. Moses* (2001)
Time entries must be sufficiently detailed to allow verification.

### *PLCM Group v. Drexler* (2000)
Attorney bears burden of proving fees are reasonable.
