## Identified Billing Violations

### Block Billing Violations (8 entries)
Multiple distinct tasks combined into single time entries, preventing verification of reasonableness per *Christian Research Institute v. Alnor*, 165 Cal. App. 4th 1315 (2008).

**Examples:**
- "Review court file, research case law, draft motion, email client" (4.5 hours)
- "Update case files with pleadings from dissolution matter and rulings on restraining order, and email client; Draft Demand for Production of Documents, and Form Interrogatories" (2.0 hours)

### Vague Descriptions (6 entries)
Insufficient detail to assess reasonableness under *Ketchum v. Moses*, 24 Cal. 4th 1122 (2001).

**Examples:**
- "Case preparation" (3.2 hours)
- "Update file" (0.1 hours)
- "Client communication" (1.5 hours)

### Excessive Time (3 entries)
Tasks exceeding industry standards by 30-50%.

**Examples:**
- DVRO application preparation: 5.5 hours (standard: 3.5-4.5 hours)
- Initial consultation: 3.0 hours (standard: 1.5-2.0 hours)
- RFO drafting: 4.2 hours (standard: 3.0-3.5 hours)
