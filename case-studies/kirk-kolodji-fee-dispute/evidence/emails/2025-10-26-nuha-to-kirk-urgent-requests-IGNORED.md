## Legal Analysis

### Rule 1.4: Communication Duty

California Rules of Professional Conduct, Rule 1.4(a)(3) requires attorney to:
> "keep the client reasonably informed about significant developments relating to the representation"

AND Rule 1.4(a)(4) requires attorney to:
> "promptly comply with reasonable requests for information"

**Application:** Kirk violated both provisions by:
1. Failing to respond to three explicit client requests
2. Failing to inform client of decision to ignore income correction request
3. Filing motions without confirming client understood and approved content

### Rule 1.2: Scope of Representation

Rule 1.2(a) requires attorney to:
> "abide by a client's decisions concerning the objectives of representation"

**Application:** Income reporting is factual representation affecting case outcome (spousal support). Client's instruction to report $0 income (accurate fact) should have been followed.

### Malpractice Elements

1. **Duty:** Kirk owed Nuha duty of competent representation
2. **Breach:** Failed to follow client instruction on FL-150, ignored urgent email
3. **Causation:** Incorrect income reporting directly undermines spousal support case
4. **Damages:** Reduced spousal support award (quantifiable after hearing)
